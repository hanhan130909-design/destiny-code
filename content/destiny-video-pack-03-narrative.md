## PRODUCTION NOTES

### Color Grade Reference
- **Shadows:** Desaturated, pushed toward deep navy/black
- **Midtones:** Cool-neutral with 5% warmth
- **Highlights:** Warm amber-gold (#D4A853 approximate)
- **Film Grain:** 8% overlay, 35mm grain plate
- **Contrast:** High contrast, crushed blacks at 5%

### Font Selection
- On-screen text: Use a clean geometric sans-serif (e.g., Inter, Helvetica Neue Light) for data overlays
- Destiny Code logo: Custom serif or high-end serif (e.g., Cormorant Garamond, Playfair Display)
- Size: Minimum 28pt for readability on mobile
- Animation: Fade-in with 0.3s ease, subtle 2px vertical drift

### Voiceover Recording Notes
- Mic: Large diaphragm condenser (Neumann U87 or equivalent)
- Distance: 6-8 inches from mic
- Delivery: Measured. Slightly slower than conversational. Let words land.
- Processing chain: Subtle compression (2:1 ratio) → light EQ (boost 120Hz, cut 400Hz, air at 12kHz) → plate reverb (18% wet, 1.2s decay) → subtle tape saturation
- Reference voices: Peter Coyote, Werner Herzog (English narration), David Attenborough
