## Q2: "How accurate is Chinese astrology compared to Western?"

**Answer:**

BaZi excels at **timing and elemental dynamics** — it tells you *when* to act and *what energies* are working for or against you.

**The best approach I've seen:** use both. BaZi for timing major decisions (career changes, investments, moves). Western for understanding your psychological patterns.
