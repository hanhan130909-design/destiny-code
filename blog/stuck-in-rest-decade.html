The 10-Year Chapters You Never Chose

BaZi divides your life into Luck Pillars — approximately 10-year segments, each governed by a different elemental combination. These aren't metaphors. They're the literal energetic weather system you're living inside, determined by your birth chart's interaction with the flowing time stem-branch cycle. Your Luck Cycle architecture was set the moment you were born.

Some Luck Pillars are Output Decades: the element that drives expression, production, wealth generation, and visibility is dominant. These are the decades where hustle works — because the environment is cooperating with the effort.

Other Luck Pillars are Resource Decades: the element that nurtures, studies, restores, and builds internal reserves is dominant. These decades feel slow. Career momentum stalls. External rewards diminish. You are not broken — you are in a planting season disguised as a failure.

When you force output during a Resource decade, you don't just fail to produce — you deplete reserves you'll need later. This is why some people emerge from a Rest decade and suddenly explode into success in the next pillar. They didn't get lucky. They spent the planting season actually planting — while everyone else was screaming at seeds for not being trees yet. The Four Pillars don't reward impatience. They reward alignment.
