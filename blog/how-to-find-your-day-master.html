What is the Day Master?

In BaZi, your birth chart contains four pillars — Year, Month, Day, and Hour. Each pillar has a Heavenly Stem and an Earthly Branch. The Heavenly Stem of your Day Pillar is your Day Master (日主). How to Find Your Day Master

Your Day Master is calculated from your birth date using the Chinese sexagenary cycle — a 60-day calendar that has run continuously for millennia. The calculation involves:

  1. Your Gregorian birth date (year, month, day)
  2. A reference date (January 1, 1900 = Jia Xu day)
  3. Counting the days between your birth and the reference
  4. Applying modulo 10 to find your Heavenly Stem
