# Destiny Code — Lead Magnet & EDM Funnel Strategy

## Lead Magnet Definition

### What the User Gets (Free)
"Your Element Snapshot" — A 2-page PDF delivered via email containing:

1. **Your Dominant Element** — Which of the Five Elements (Wood, Fire, Earth, Metal, Water) rules your birth chart, and what it means for your personality
2. **Your Day Master Type** — The core energy archetype you embody (framed as "Your Cosmic Personality Type," akin to MBTI)
3. **One Key Insight** — A single, specific, intriguing observation about your chart (e.g., "Your chart shows a powerful creative channel that peaks in autumn")
4. **The Tease** — A preview of what the full 20-page Personal Energy Blueprint would reveal ("This is just the surface. Your complete blueprint maps your career arc, relationship patterns, and the next 12 months in detail.")

### Why This Lead Magnet Works
- Low friction: Just enter birth details (users LOVE talking about themselves)
- Immediate value: They learn something new about themselves in 24 hours
- Curiosity gap: The snapshot reveals enough to validate, not enough to satisfy
- Shareability: "I'm a Fire Day Master" is conversation-worthy (organic word-of-mouth)

## 7-Email Nurture Sequence (Detailed)

### Email 0: Instant Confirmation (Immediately After Form Submit)

Subject: "Your cosmic coordinates are being decoded ☯"

Content Outline:
- Thank you for sharing your birth details
- "Our system is analyzing your unique energy configuration — this takes a few hours to do properly"
- What to expect: Your free Element Snapshot arrives within 24 hours
- Set expectations: Check spam folder, whitelist this address
- Small tease: "While you wait — did you know only 18% of people share your Day Master type?"
- PS: "No spam, ever. Just your blueprint."

---

### Email 1: Free Element Snapshot Delivery (Day 0-1, within 24 hours)

Subject: "[Name], your Element Snapshot is ready ✨"

Content Outline:
- Big reveal: "You are a [Element] type, with a [Day Master] core energy"
- 1-sentence summary of what this means about their personality
- Link to download the 2-page PDF
- The curiosity hook: "This snapshot covers about 10% of what your birth chart contains. Want to know what the other 90% reveals?"
- Preview bullets for full report: Career path, relationship patterns, 12-month forecast, growth themes
- CTA: "Curious about the full picture? → [Link to full report page]"
- Social proof placeholder: "Over 500 people have discovered their blueprint this month"

---

### Email 2: Deep Dive — Your Element Explained (Day 3)

Subject: "What being a [Element] type REALLY means"

Content Outline:
- Gratitude hook: "Now that you've had time to sit with your Element Snapshot..."
- Deep dive into their specific element:
  - Fire types: charisma, passion, burnout risk, need for rest cycles
  - Water types: intuition, depth, tendency to absorb others' emotions
  - Wood types: growth-oriented, visionary, impatience challenge
  - Metal types: precision, justice, rigidity trap
  - Earth types: stability, nurturing, over-giving pattern
- Relatable examples: "Ever notice how you [specific behavior]? That's your [Element] energy."
- Bridge to pain point: "When your element is out of balance, you might feel [symptom]. Your full blueprint shows how to restore that balance."
- CTA: Soft — "Your full blueprint maps all your elements, not just one."

## Tool Setup: Mailchimp Free Plan

Mailchimp's free tier (as of 2025-2026):
- Up to 500 contacts
- Up to 1,000 sends/month (2,500/month on some plans)
- Basic automations (welcome email + simple sequences)
- Landing page builder (not needed — you have Vercel)

### Setup Checklist
1. Create Mailchimp account (free tier)
2. Set up Audience ("Destiny Code Subscribers")
3. Configure "Welcome Automation" for Email 0
4. Build 7-email sequence as "Customer Journey" or "Classic Automation"
5. Add signup form embed code to Vercel landing page OR use Mailchimp API via webhook
6. Set up tags for segmentation: `element_fire`, `element_water`, etc. (future personalization)
7. Test the full flow with your own email before going live
8. Monitor deliverability: check spam score at mail-tester.com

### Fallback Tool: ConvertKit Free Plan
- Up to 1,000 subscribers
- Visual automation builder (easier for complex sequences)
- Better for creator/coach businesses than Mailchimp
- Limited to one sequence on free plan (enough for now)
