## Production Tips (No Design Skills Needed)

1. **Use CapCut (free)** — Templates for text animations, auto-captions, transitions
2. **Background footage** — Free from Pexels.com / Pixabay.com (search: "stars," "galaxy," "meditation," "nature")
3. **Music** — TikTok's built-in sound library, search "lo-fi," "ambient," "healing frequencies," "432Hz"
4. **Text overlays** — CapCut's "cinematic text" presets. Gold/white on dark background
5. **The logo** — Create a simple ☯ symbol animation (free on Canva) and paste it at the end of every video
6. **Batch record** — Film all talking-head audio in one session, then layer visuals separately. Much faster.
