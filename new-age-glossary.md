## HOW TO USE THIS TABLE

- **Column 1 (Chinese 原名)** — the original BaZi term. For internal reference and credibility.
- **Column 2 (直译 · DO NOT USE)** — the literal translation. Included so your team knows what to avoid. These terms (especially "Killing", "Rob", "Hurting", "Punishment", "Destruction") carry fear-based or aggressive connotations that violate the Destiny Code brand voice.
- **Column 3 (New Age Expression)** — the production-ready English term. Use these in all customer-facing content: reports, emails, landing pages, social media.
