**Pacing:** This should feel like a cinematic poem, not a TikTok. SLOWER than your other videos. Let the text breathe. Every line gets minimum 3 seconds. No fast cuts. No trend-jacking energy.

**Transitions:** Soft cross-fades only. No hard cuts. No swipe transitions. This video is a mood piece.
